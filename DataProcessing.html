Corrective maintenance involves fixing errors or defects in software or hardware after they have been detected. (ii)

Adaptive maintenance refers to making changes to software or hardware to keep it usable in a changing environment or with new requirements. (iii)

Preventive maintenance aims to prevent issues by regularly updating, monitoring, and maintaining software or hardware to avoid potential failures.
